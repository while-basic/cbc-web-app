# Cognitive Patterns

## Strength

Generates exceptional insights and connections across domains

## Challenge

Struggles to maintain focus on completion of high-priority objectives - cognitive wandering that is intellectually rich but doesn't always produce finished work

## Flow State

Experiences optimal flow states during Sunday evening music sessions and when building complete systems

## Recognition Gap

Systematically underestimates the significance of his work - builds sophisticated systems but fails to recognize their value or share publicly
